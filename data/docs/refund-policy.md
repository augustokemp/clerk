# Refund Policy

## Request window
Customers may request a refund within 14 days of payment confirmation. After
this window, refunds are granted only for defective or damaged items.

## Refunds for bank transfer payments
Payments made by bank transfer are refunded within 2 business days, returned
directly to the account that originated the transaction. A transfer cannot be
refunded to a different account than the one used for payment.

## Refunds for credit card payments
Credit card reversals appear on the customer's statement within up to 2 billing
cycles, depending on the card issuer. The full amount is always refunded.

## Orders already shipped
If the order has already shipped, the refund is only processed after the product
returns to the distribution center and passes an integrity check.
