# Payments FAQ

## My bank transfer hasn't been confirmed yet. What should I do?
Bank transfers usually confirm within a few minutes. If your order still shows
"awaiting payment" after 30 minutes, check that your bank generated a receipt.
Unidentified payments are automatically cancelled after 24 hours, with no charge
to the customer.

## Can I pay in installments?
Yes. Credit card payments can be split into up to 12 installments. Plans longer
than 3 installments may include interest, shown before you confirm the order.
Bank transfers are always paid in full.

## Which card brands are accepted?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.

## Is it safe to pay on the website?
Yes. Card details are processed directly by the payment gateway and are never
stored on our servers. The connection is secured with TLS.

## Can I change the payment method after checkout?
No. Once an order is placed, you must cancel it and place a new order with the
new payment method.
