# Shipping and Delivery

## Delivery windows
- Standard shipping: 3 to 7 business days.
- Express shipping: 1 to 3 business days.
- International shipping: 7 to 15 business days, depending on customs.

The delivery window starts only after **payment confirmation**, not at checkout.
Bank transfers usually confirm within minutes; credit card payments may take a
few hours for authorization.

## How to track an order
Customers track their order using the tracking number sent by email and
available in the "My Orders" area of their account.

## What each status means
- **Processing**: the order has been paid and is being prepared for shipment.
- **Shipped**: the order has left the warehouse and has a tracking code.
- **Delivered**: the order was received at the registered address.
- **Returned**: the carrier could not deliver and the order came back.
