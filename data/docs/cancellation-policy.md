# Cancellation Policy

## Cancelling before shipment
While an order is in the "processing" status, the customer can cancel it free of
charge from the "My Orders" area. The refund is processed in full, following the
Refund Policy.

## Cancelling after shipment
Once an order reaches the "shipped" status, it can no longer be cancelled online.
The customer must refuse delivery or request a return after delivery, within the
14-day window.

## Processing time
Cancellations requested before shipment are processed within 1 business day. The
payment reversal follows the timing of the original payment method (bank transfer
within 2 business days; credit card within up to 2 billing cycles).

## Cancellation due to out-of-stock items
If an item becomes unavailable after purchase, the order is cancelled by the store
and the amount is refunded in full, with an email notification to the customer.
